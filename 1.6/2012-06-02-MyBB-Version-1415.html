MyBB 1.4.15 was released on 22 February 2011.

MyBB 1.4.15 is now available on the MyBB website and is a security update to the MyBB 1.4 series. MyBB 1.6.2 was released simultaneously, fixing the same security issues and providing a few bug fixes for the MyBB 1.6 series.

More information on the MyBB Blog: http://blog.mybb.com/2011/02/22/mybb-1-6-2-and-1-4-15-security-update/
