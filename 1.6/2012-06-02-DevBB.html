DevBB was the predecessor of MyBB, developed in the transition between the breakup of the XMB group and the release of MyBB.

DevBB is no longer supported and is not even available for download anywhere. Anyone using it are recommended to upgrade to the latest version of MyBB due to security risks and bugs present in DevBB.

  • DevBB Final - 1 April 2002
  • DevBB :: Stardust - 21 May 2002 - Bug fix release
  • DevBB :: Special Edition - 23 June 2003 - Last release of the DevBB series
