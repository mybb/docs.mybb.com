The MyBB Documentation is split up into two, corresponding to the two major components of MyBB:

The Front End comprises of all of the pages that visitors on your site see. Some pages are restricted to registered users, and moderators. All the front end pages are generated from the MyBB theme and templates system. The Admin CP is the back end of the board where administrators can configure and manage the board.
