MyBB 1.4.8 was released on 26 June 2009.

MyBB 1.4.8 is a general maintenance & security update to the MyBB 1.4 series. It fixes 1 medium risk and 1 low risk security vulnerability. We recommend everybody upgrades to this release immediately or patch their boards with the manual patching instructions below.

This vulnerability affects MyBB 1.4.7. In addition, a MyBB 1.2 patch can be found in the follow link.

More information on the MyBB Blog: http://blog.mybb.com/2009/06/26/mybb-148-released-maintenance-security-release/
