MyBB 1.4.16 was released on 17 April 2011.

MyBB 1.4.16 is now available on the MyBB website and is a security update to the MyBB 1.4 series. MyBB 1.6.3 was released simultaneously, fixing the same security issues for the MyBB 1.6 series.

More information on the MyBB Blog: http://blog.mybb.com/2011/04/17/mybb-1-6-3-and-1-4-16-security-update/
