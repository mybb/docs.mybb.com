Release Date

MyBB 1.6.14 was released on 30 June 2014.

Announcement Summary

Overview

MyBB 1.6.14 is a security and maintenance release and fixes 5 vulnerabilities and 50 reported issues.

Fixed Issues

  • Vulnerabilities:
    • Medium Risk: Possibility of executing PHP code through settings
    • Low Risk: A XSS vulnerability in polls.php
    • Low Risk: A XSS vulnerability in portal.php
    • Low Risk: Password protected forums can be viewed from the portal
    • Low Risk: Super moderators have more permissions than expected
  • Fixed issues in 1.6.14
  • Unfixed issues
