Product Management

Management team

These people manage MyBB as a whole keeping it tip-top in shape and function. Members of this team may also be members of other teams, but are in the Management group on the Community Forums. These members are marked with an asterisk (*) when listed in their respective teams.

Development Team

Development team

Currently, work is being done on the next version of MyBB 1.6, 1.6.6, as well as MyBB 2.0.0. See Development for more information. You can check the developer's blogs for more details, hints, and previews. You may also be interested in the versioning system.

Public Relations Liaison

Public Relations Liaison

The Public Relations Liaisons or the PR Team helps to keep the public up-to-date with news and announcements via Twitter, Facebook, blog posts and mailing lists. They also help out in the community by giving support and helping with any problems that may arise.

SQA Team

Software Quality Assurance Team

The SQA Team, or Software Quality Assurance Team, are in charge of ensuring bug fixes are fixed, checking all commits, and ensuring the general quality of the software.

Support Team

Support team

These are the people who dedicate time helping MyBB users out with problems on their forum. They give support on the Community Forums, and maintain this Wiki.


Contact

Individual team members may be contacted via the links in their forum profile. Please use the Community Forums for support, rather than contacting the team members individually (unless they have asked you first to do so).

To contact the MyBB Group in general, email: contact <at> mybb <dot> com For security related matters, please use: security <at> mybb <dot> com
